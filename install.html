Fraction Calculator Mobile App

Get instant access to our professional fraction calculator designed for contractors. Works offline once installed.

  • Mixed number calculations (e.g., 1 1/4 + 2 3/8)
  • Precise decimal results
  • Fast, mobile-optimized interface

Open Calculator / Install

On Android/Desktop you’ll see an install prompt. On iPhone/iPad, follow the steps below.

Tip: If this opens inside another app (Facebook/Instagram), tap the •••/Share menu and choose Open in Safari/Chrome first—then follow the steps below.

How to install on iPhone / iPad

Open the calculator link above in Safari.

Tap the Share icon at the bottom of Safari.

Scroll and tap Add to Home Screen.

Tap Add. The app will appear on your Home Screen and work offline.

How to install on Android / Desktop

Open the calculator link above.

In Chrome/Edge, tap the menu ⋮ (or address-bar icon) and choose Install app or Add to Home screen.

Confirm Install. The app icon will appear with your other apps and work offline.

Having trouble? Make sure you’re on an https:// page and not inside an in-app browser.

What you’ll get

  • Two-column display: full equation on the left, results on the right
  • Mixed result and decimal (no improper fraction)
  • History with running totals
  • Works offline once installed
